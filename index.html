About Campus X

An elite program trains children to think like scientists, create like engineers, and apply design thinking — grooming the next Elon Musk. In the 3 months junior researcher program, we'll share our experience designing the Rice robot, building it, and bringing it to market.

About Rice Robotics

Rice Robotics, a Hong Kong-based commercial robotics startup, was founded in 2020 by young entrepreneurs from UC Berkeley and CUHK. Backed by investors like Alibaba and New World, the company has established a strong presence in the Japanese market, with 500 deployments across Hong Kong and Japan, including partnerships with renowned establishments like K11 and SoftBank. Setting itself apart, Rice Robotics is the only local company to own an R&D and manufacturing site in the heart of Hong Kong. Junior researchers are invited to visit and explore the exciting world of robotics, shaping the next frontier of technology.
